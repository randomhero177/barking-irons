Anti Merch

In 2009 Barking Irons began collaborating with music artists to redefine the creatively dead category ofartist merch. Barking Irons creates immersive limited edition collections for artists and iconic brands that can tell a great story through images, clothing, and culture. We look to connect the past to the now by broadcasting those who have defied their times.
