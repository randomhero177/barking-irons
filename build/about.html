Anti Established In spring 2003 Barking Irons was borne on the Bowery of downtown New York City. Since then the brand, led by Brothers Daniel and Michael Casarella, has forged a long history built on peculiar American stories told through clothing and accessories.

Anti Merch In 2009 Barking Irons began collaborating with music artists to redefine the creatively dead category ofartist merch. Barking Irons creates immersive limited edition collections for artists and iconic brands that can tell a great story through images, clothing, and culture. We look to connect the past to the now by broadcasting those who have defied their times.
