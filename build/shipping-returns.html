General Order Info

  • All received orders will be shipped within 72 hours
  • Orders shipping within the USA / Canada / Mexico will receive a flat rate shipping & handling fee of $7.00 USD.
  • We are happy to accept Returns & Exchanges provided the merchandise is un-used, un-washed, un-altered, condition.
  • All orders shipping outside of North America will receive a flat rate shipping & handling fee of $45 USD.

Returns & Exchanges

Returned merchandise will be accepted in un-used, un-washed, un-altered condition within 14 days of receipt of your order.

Exchanged merchandise will be accepted in un-used, un-washed, un-altered condition within 30 days of receipt of your order. Please allow 48-72 orders for new merchandise to be shipped.

Returned & Exchanged merchandise should be returned to the below address at customer’s expense* We highly recommend you use a trackable or insured service when returning merchandise.

*For Returns or Exchanges that are the result of defective merchandise or receiving an incorrect order Return shipping service will be paid by Barking Irons.

Procedure for Returning Merchandise:

  • Merchandise must be un-used, un-washed, un-altered in any way and deemed New (as shipped) upon receipt at our facility.
  • Pack and seal in same or equivalent packaging as it was received.
  • Return items to the below address postage paid:

Barking Irons
c/o Hifi Entertainment

Questions about Returns & Exchanges? email myorder@barkingirons.com

International Shipping

International orders shipping to locations outside of North America are covered under our International Flat Rate of $45 USD. Please allow up to two weeks transit time for international orders.

Barking Irons reserves the right to refuse international orders that require higher than average shipping charges.

International returns or exchanges are subject to the same polices as domestic orders.

International shipping questions? Contact myorder@barkingirons.com

There is something wrong with my order

If you received your order and something is not quite as you ordered it, please contact us immediately and someone will get back to you shortly.

For any defective or damaged merchandise, or if did not receive the correct items per your order confirmation, Barking Irons will send you a return shipping label and work to resolve the issue quickly.

Please note Barking Irons is only responsible to ship orders as they appear on the order confirmation.

If you notice an error on your order confirmation please contact us immediately and we will try to amend before your items ship.

Need to discuss a problem with your order? email myorder@barkingirons.com

Billing Questions

If you have questions about your invoice, charges, or a pending refund please contact billing@barkingirons.com

Please allow 72 hours for refunds to fully process and may take time to appear on your statement.

Refunds will only be processed once returned or cancelled items have been received and verified.
